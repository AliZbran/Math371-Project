Introduction

In this project, we will talk about One Dimensional Mass Balance of a Reactor. Mass balance helps us to know all the unknown at the feed or which produce from the reactor in the system. We asked to find the concentration along the longitudinal axis of a rectangular reactor. The system could be at a steady-state or unsteady state. In our project, we will solve the system at an unsteady state which is dependent on time which will lead us to solve the system with partial differential equations.
