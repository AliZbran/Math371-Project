FUNDAMENTALS

Two diffrenets types of processes could be anlayzed regarding the unsteady state condition:

a) Batch process
All reactants fed to the reactor at the beginning of the process and the products are removed when the reaction is complete. No reactants are fed to the reactor and no product is removed during the process.

b) semi-Batch process

A semi-batch process is when reactants are periodically added or products are periodically removed. processes as neither batch nor continuous.
